Accordion

        An accordion menu is a vertically stacked list of headers that can be clicked to reveal or hide content associated with them. It is one of many ways you can expose content to users in a progressive manner. 
        Allowing people to have control over the content by expanding it or deferring it for later lets them decide what to read and what to ignore. By allowing people to control what content they see and what remains hidden, 
        the information feels less overwhelming.
