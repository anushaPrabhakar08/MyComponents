Notification

Notiﬁcation banner communicates the state of the entire application or a section of it. It stays visible until the user interacts with it but banners can automatically be dismissed in-case they are providing non-critical information.
Banner notiﬁcations are typically placed over the full width of the application (mobile) or at a speciﬁc/full width at the local level of a application. They can be used on mobile, tablets and desktops.
